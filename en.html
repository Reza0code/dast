About us

We are a group of volunteers who created the Dast-Yari charity fund to support people in need and families in vulnerable situations.

Our work is based on compassion, trust, responsibility, and transparency. We believe that even small contributions, when made with good intentions and cooperation, can bring hope and comfort back into people’s lives.

Our purpose

Helping those who truly need support

Dast-Yari focuses on necessary, urgent, and humanitarian help — support that can bring hope during difficult times.

Membership

Membership in Dast-Yari is a voluntary humanitarian commitment to support people in need.

Minimum monthly contribution Members living in Sweden or Norway contribute at least 100 kronor per month. Members in countries using euro, dollar, or franc contribute at least 10 in their local currency, for example 10 € / 10 $ / 10 CHF.
